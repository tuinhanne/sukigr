We are sukigroup

With 5 operating companies, 14 entities worldwide, 17 locations, over 120 years of experience, 1,000 engaged employees and a range of 60,000 products, we supply millions of people across Europe with DIY and craftsmen products. Our products help them to build, renovate, repair and beautify their homes, flats, gardens and favorite places.

Who is sukigroup

A strong team developing innovative solutions – globally present, locally anchored and driven by growth through efficient processes and products.
